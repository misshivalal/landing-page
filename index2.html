Format and Rules of Contest

  • 1: This contest is open only to students in classes 6-8 from Etah City.
  • 2: Each participant is allowed only one submission.
  • 3: To participate:
  • - Complete the entry form.
  • - After submitting the form, read the passage provided via the link.
  • - Write the passage on paper, take a clear photo of your handwritten text, and upload it on the online submission page.
  • 4 Guidelines:
  • - Use only black or blue ink.
  • - Write on a plain white A4 sheet or notebook (plain or ruled), free of prints, pictures, or decorations, using only one page.
  • - Use the same writing instrument throughout the entry.
  • - Writing may be in cursive or simple.
  • 5: The decisions of the contest will be final by the judges.
  • 6: All contest entries will become the property of the organizers (Shivalal Notebooks), who reserve the right to publish, print, or advertise them.
  • 7: By participating, you agree to the contest rules and accept the judges' decisions.
